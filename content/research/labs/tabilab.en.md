## Description

TABILAB's purpose is to conduct research on natural language processing,
information retrieval, and bioinformatics. TABILAB is mainly funded by the
Boğaziçi University Research Fund, the Scientific and Technical Research Council
of Turkey (TUBITAK), and the European Commission.
