Our 2026 Spring Computer Engineering senior students successfully presented the poster and demo sessions of more than 60 graduation projects, marking one of our department's most anticipated traditions.

A jury composed of industry representatives, faculty members, and PhD students evaluated the projects throughout the day. Following the jury's evaluation, the award-winning projects of this term are as follows:

**First Place**

- Project: Multiple Odor Source Localization in 3D Using Sniffing
- Students: Ali Sönmez, Efe Feyzi Mantaroğlu
- Advisors: H. Birkan Yılmaz, Emre Uğur

**Second Place**

- Project: SelVeri: Self-Verifying Programming Language
- Students: Yağız Kaan Aydoğdu, Yusuf Demir
- Advisor: Doğan Ulus

**Third Place (Tie, Group 1)**

- Project: AI-Powered Personalized Pronunciation Assessment Platform
- Students: Ümit Can Evleksiz, Ömer Faruk Bayram
- Advisors: Lale Akarun, Murat Saraçlar

**Third Place (Tie, Group 2)**

- Project: TALİMAT: Culturally Grounded Synthetic Instruction Fine-Tuning Dataset Generation for Turkish
- Student: Muhammed Emin Arayıcı
- Advisor: Suzan Üsküdarlı

We thank all of our graduating students and their advisors for making our department proud with their visionary work, and we wish them continued success in their academic and professional lives.
