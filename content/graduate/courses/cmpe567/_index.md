## Catalog Description

Concepts and research topics in emerging wireless broadband networks. Wireless local area networks, wireless metropolitan area networks, wireless regional area networks. Cognitive radio with emphasis on sensing, mobility, and seamless operation.

## Course Learning Outcomes

- Understand concepts in emerging broadband wireless networks  
- Learn fundamentals of WLANs, WMANs, and WRANs  
- Understand cognitive radio principles including sensing and mobility  
- Analyze seamless operation challenges in wireless broadband systems
