## Catalog Description

The widening of students' perceptiveness and awareness of topics of interest to computer engineers through seminars offered by faculty, guest speakers, and graduate students.

## Course Learning Outcomes

- Gain exposure to current research topics in Computer Engineering  
- Develop awareness of emerging technologies and research directions  
- Learn from faculty, guest speakers, and peers through seminar presentations  
- Improve academic presentation and discussion skills
