## Catalog Description

Signal terminology. Empirical modeling and approximation. Probability concept and signal characteristics for classification. Random processes and decision making. Fundamentals of learning. Optimization theory in classification. Information theory and fuzzy concept applications.

## Course Learning Outcomes

- Understand signal terminology and empirical modeling  
- Learn approximation techniques relevant to AI  
- Understand probability concepts for classification  
- Analyze random processes for decision making  
- Learn fundamentals of machine learning  
- Understand optimization theory in classification tasks  
- Gain familiarity with information theory and fuzzy concepts in AI
