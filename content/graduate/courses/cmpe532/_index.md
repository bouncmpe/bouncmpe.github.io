## Catalog Description

Man-machine communication. Speech models and representations. Speech synthesis. Speech coding. Speech recognition. Dynamic Time Warping and Hidden Markov Models. Neural networks for speech processing. Speech enhancement.

## Course Learning Outcomes

- Understand the fundamentals of speech processing and man-machine communication  
- Learn speech models and representations  
- Understand techniques for speech synthesis and coding  
- Learn fundamentals of speech recognition  
- Understand Dynamic Time Warping and Hidden Markov Models  
- Learn neural network approaches for speech processing  
- Gain familiarity with speech enhancement techniques
