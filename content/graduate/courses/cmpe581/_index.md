## Catalog Description

Study of special topics in computer engineering. The specific topic is chosen from among the recent technological or theoretical developments in mobile and wireless networks.

## Course Learning Outcomes

- Understand advanced concepts in mobile and wireless networking  
- Gain exposure to current research topics in the field  
- Learn how emerging technologies influence computer engineering solutions  
- Explore specialized problems and applications within wireless systems
