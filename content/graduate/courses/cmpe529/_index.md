## Catalog Description

Theory of serializability. Various concurrency control algorithms and their proofs of correctness. Recovery in centralized and distributed databases.

## Course Learning Outcomes

- Understand the theory of serializability  
- Learn major concurrency control algorithms  
- Analyze proofs of correctness for concurrency mechanisms  
- Understand recovery concepts in centralized databases  
- Learn recovery techniques for distributed databases
